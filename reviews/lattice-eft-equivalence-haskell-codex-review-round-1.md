## Findings

1. **`Monoid.hs:177` (`eqG`) â€” witness-search completeness is a convention, not
   enforced by the type.** `eqG` only searches `zero : cWitnesses`, but the
   Grothendieck relation in the paper quantifies over any `e in M`. This is
   complete for the shipped monoids only by construction, not by the `CMonoid`
   type itself. Fix: hide the `CMonoid` constructor behind certified
   constructors (only `freeCMonoid`/`toyMonoid` produce values), or rename to
   bounded equality with a documented witness set.

2. **`Monoid.hs:86` (`scale`) â€” violates the nonnegative-exponent invariant.**
   `scale :: Integer -> Elt -> Elt` accepts negative multipliers;
   `scale (-1) (gen "a")` constructs an invalid carrier element (the module's
   own Haddock says all exponents are nonnegative). Fix with `Natural`,
   `NonNegative Integer`, or a checked smart constructor.

3. **`Monoid.hs:123` (`freeCMonoid`) â€” silently discards its generator list.**
   `freeCMonoid _gs` ignores its argument, so `freeCMonoid ["a"]` still accepts
   `gen "b"` and behaves like a larger free monoid than declared. Fix by
   storing/validating the generator set, or remove the parameter and document
   the unrestricted free monoid on all `String` labels.

4. **`Monoid.hs:193,205` (`Hom`/`induced`) and `Proofs.hs:140` â€” no
   relation-respecting check.** `Hom = Elt -> Integer` and `induced` accept any
   function, including ones that do not respect the monoid's relations;
   `Proofs.hs:140` even demonstrates the resulting ill-defined case. Fix with a
   `MonoidHom` newtype/smart constructor carrying the source `CMonoid` and a
   relation-respecting certificate/check, and turn the ill-defined
   demonstration into a rejection test.

5. **`Monoid.hs:27` and `Monoid.hs:134` â€” stale Haddock.** Both comments say the
   toy monoid's group completion "collapses to 0", but the paper (and the
   code's own behavior) says `K(M) ~= Z`, generated by `[t]`, with only `[a]=0`.
   Fix both comments to match `papers/latex/lattice-eft-equivalence.tex:666`.

6. **`Properties.hs:85` â€” toy-monoid `eqG` path lacks equivalence/congruence
   coverage.** Group laws (`prop_gAssoc` etc.) are only QuickChecked over
   `free3`; the non-cancellative witness-search path in `eqG toyMonoid` has no
   dedicated tests. Add toy-monoid `eqG` reflexive/symmetric/transitive checks,
   `plusG` congruence checks, and group laws under `toyMonoid`.

7. **`Properties.hs:179` (`prop_landmarks`) â€” not a full Table 1 regression.**
